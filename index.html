Work:

As a business partner at Delpick, I continually implement innovative ideas to enrich and grow the company. About The Company:

Delpick, Inc. is an online marketplace that lists products available at local retailers, in real time. Shoppers can search and buy products offered by local retailers on the Delpick website/apps and choose from 3 different pick up/delivery options. Shoppers can choose to pick up the products at the local retailer location. Shoppers can choose to get their products delivered within 1-hour Shoppers can choose to get their products within a specific delivery time-window We have also designed and manufactured our own tamper proof, unique QR coded/ID delivery bags in addition to securing an exclusive US distribution agreement with our manufacture.
